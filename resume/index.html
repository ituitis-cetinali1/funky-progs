About Me

I graduated from Istanbul Technical University in 2023 and started to work on web development.

I started my business by building several websites to local enterprises when I was a student

By the time i graduated I was working on developing mobile apps myself and that is where I learned the importance of personal information.

After finding out what kinds of tricks the big companies do to get peoples personal info, I decided to do something about it!

At first I decided to make it more clear to users that you can say no to companies tracking your info and I developed the tracker-tracker application. I made people know how they can turn the tracking off and which sites or apps were tracking them until then.

After finding out how some websites refuse to turn off their tracking by claiming "bugs", I decided to develop the tracker-tricker, the website that gives the other websites the wrong idea about you.
